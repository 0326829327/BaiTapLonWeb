Every time you shop, you save. That’s what we call a shopping win-win.

Off-price? On point.

We’re what you call an off-price retailer. For us, true value means the perfect combination of brand, fashion, price, and quality. We Buy Smart (So You Can Shop Smart)
We seek out all kinds of vendors: big brands to boutique, coveted designers, as well as up-and-coming labels you need to know about. There’s also some merchandise we design or have manufactured for us in order to bring you the best quality fashion at an amazing price.
For example, we work with artisans to source beautiful, hand-crafted mirrors and wooden accent pieces from India, amazing cashmere and merino wool from Italy, and soaps that are produced in the United Kingdom, Portugal, and Australia.
Our team of buyers are both opportunistic and entrepreneurial, so when a designer overproduces, a manufacturer makes too much product, or other stores overbuy, we step in and negotiate the lowest possible price and pass the savings on to you. Most of our products are current season styles, some are past, others are timeless classics. But no matter the find, it’s always priced just right.
